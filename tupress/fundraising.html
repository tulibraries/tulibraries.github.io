SUPPORT TEMPLE UNIVERSITY PRESS

As the independent publisher of Temple University, Temple University Press is committed to a mission of urban education and passionate scholarship. Our goal is to perpetuate Temple’s tradition of urban education and academic excellence and to bring new authors and new ideas to an ever-wider audience. Gifts from individuals, foundations and corporations play a vital role in making this goal a reality. You can play your part by making a gift today.

Donors to Temple University Press receive benefits such as complimentary books, invitations to special events, and opportunities to meet and talk to Press authors and editors. Click here for the complete list of donor benefits.

There are several ways you can help:

Make a gift to the Temple University Press Fund

By making a gift to our general fund, your donation goes to support all the work we do at Temple University Press, from academic titles by top scholars in fields such as sociology, criminology, political science and Asian American Studies, to great regional titles like the Philadelphia Murals and the Stories They Tell and A Guide to the Great Gardens of the Philadelphia Region.

Support a Specific Title:

Each year, Temple University Press produces books whose publication would be difficult, or sometimes impossible, without the support of outside contributors. By becoming a Publishing Patron, you can help make sure we can continue to publish high quality, innovative books.

Endow a Specific Series:

Temple University Press produces book series in a variety of subject areas. By endowing a series, you ensure its continued viability while attaching your name, in perpetuity, to a set of books that reflects your passions and interests. For a list of series currently available for endowment, click here.

Temple University Press welcomes gifts in a variety of forms and can assist you in planning your commitment to your best advantage and that of Temple University Press. Assistance is also available in including the Press in your estate through a planned gift. For further information about donating to Temple University Press, contact:
