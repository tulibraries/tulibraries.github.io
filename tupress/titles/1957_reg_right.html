About the Author(s)

Cathy Ragland is an Associate Professor of Ethnomusicology at the University of North Texas, College of Music in the Division of Music History, Theory and Ethnomusicology. She is a former music critic for the San Antonio Express-News, Seattle Times and Austin American-Statesman, where she wrote about Texas-Mexican music and m�sica norte�a, among other popular genres. She also cofounded the Mariachi Academy of New York, a community-based music program for youth in East Harlem.
