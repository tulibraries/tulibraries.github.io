Sales Representatives
 

Online ordering is easy and secure with the AAUP Secure Server. At the top left-hand corner of the individual book pages, a link reads, "Buy this Book." Simply click the link, and you'll be taken to an order page. Add the book to your shopping cart. You can check out any time you like or modify your orders in the process of browsing, and you always have the option of removing items placed in your shopping cart before placing your order.
